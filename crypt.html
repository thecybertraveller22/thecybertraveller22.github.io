Cryptography

Cryptography is an in-depth course that explores the principles and techniques used to secure information through encryption. Students will learn about the mathematical foundations of cryptography, including number theory, modular arithmetic, and prime factorization. The course covers key topics such as symmetric and asymmetric encryption, hashing algorithms, digital signatures, and cryptographic protocols. Additionally, students will examine real-world applications of cryptography in securing communications, protecting data, and ensuring privacy in digital systems. Through practical exercises and projects, students will gain hands-on experience in implementing and analyzing cryptographic systems, preparing them for challenges in the field of information security.
